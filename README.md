# 🧶 Dabble

**The guilt-free headquarters for the serially hobbied.**

A single-file web app for people who knit on Monday, pour resin on Wednesday, and discover punch needle by Friday. Track your hobby rotation, works-in-progress, supply stash, and 2am ideas — without any shame about the hibernating pile.

## Features

- **Dashboard** — active WIPs, hibernating projects, finishes, idea count, stash size, current obsession, and a Hall of Fame of finished projects
- **Spin for me 🎡** — decision paralysis? Let the wheel pick tonight's project from your in-progress and hibernating pile
- **Hobbies** — your rotation with honest statuses: Obsessed, In rotation, Resting, Retired (for now)
- **Projects** — full guilt-free lifecycle: Idea → In Progress → Hibernating 😴 → Finished 🏆 or Frogged 🐸, with progress sliders, notes, and confetti on every finish
- **Stash** — supply inventory by hobby with quantity, location, and low-stock warnings
- **Ideas** — quick-capture inbox with a Random Spark button and one-click "make it a project"

## Running it

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. That's it — no install, no build step, no server.

All data lives in your browser's `localStorage`. Use the **Export/Import backup** buttons in the footer to move your data between devices.

## Tech

One self-contained HTML file: vanilla JS, no frameworks, no dependencies, works offline.

# 🧶 Craft Club

**The guilt-free headquarters for the serially hobbied.**

A single-file web app for people who knit on Monday, pour resin on Wednesday, and discover punch needle by Friday. Explore the whole craft universe, track your hobby rotation, works-in-progress, supply stash, and 2am ideas — without any shame about the hibernating pile.

## Features

- **Explore 🧭** — a built-in catalog of **100+ crafts and hobbies** across 15 categories (fiber arts, paper, clay, resin, woodworking, kitchen crafts, maker/tech, and more), each with a difficulty rating, startup cost, and **400+ project ideas** you can save to your inbox or start as a project with one click
- **Resources 📚** — a curated library of the best places to learn, shop, and lurk for every craft category (Ravelry, DMC, CandleScience, King Arthur Baking, Printables, and friends)
- **Dashboard** — active WIPs, hibernating projects, finishes, current obsession, a rotating **Craft of the Day**, and a Hall of Fame of finished projects
- **Spin for me 🎡** — decision paralysis? Let the wheel pick tonight's project from your in-progress and hibernating pile
- **Hobbies** — your rotation with honest statuses: Obsessed, In rotation, Resting, Retired (for now)
- **Projects** — full guilt-free lifecycle: Idea → In Progress → Hibernating 😴 → Finished 🏆 or Frogged 🐸, with progress sliders, notes, status + hobby filters, and confetti on every finish
- **Stash** — supply inventory by hobby with quantity, location, and low-stock warnings
- **Ideas** — quick-capture inbox with a Random Spark button (falls back to the catalog when your inbox runs dry) and one-click "make it a project"

## Running it

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. That's it — no install, no build step, no server.

All data lives in your browser's `localStorage`. Use the **Export/Import backup** buttons in the footer to move your data between devices.

## Tech

One self-contained HTML file: vanilla JS, no frameworks, no dependencies, works offline.
